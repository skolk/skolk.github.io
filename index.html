Sean Kolk

I make tools that help communities explore new horizons. I co-founded Astraeus Ocean Systems, which builds infrastructure for coastal monitoring, and Island Lab, an experiment in developing coastal communities through gathering and design. I'm also Director of Grant Strategy at REAP Climate Center, working on climate technology and ocean systems.

I enjoy bringing people together in environments with interesting constraints. I seek the cold, water, and mountains.
