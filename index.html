Sean Kolk

Bringing people together to create more than they could have alone

Sean's a community organizer, maker, and explorer who makes tools to help communities explore new horizons.

He enjoys bringing people together in a variety of different environments to see what it means to thrive with a variety of constraints. He seeks the cold, water, and mountains.

Recent Projects:

  • Led a robotics company's business strategy from services to product
  • Coordinated the TSC founding of the open source Space ROS project in collaboration with NASA, ESA, and JAXA
  • Sailed from San Francisco Bay to the Sea of Cortez, Mexico in a 36 foot sailboat with Admiral Kate
  • Founded long running community events (Adventure Report breakfast series)
  • Built the next generation of 3D microscope devices with 3Scan (now Strateos)
  • Created a tiny house out of a shipping container
  • Co-founded an off-grid sanitation company, still operating in Kenya

Current Projects:

  • A calendar tool to help plan the next year
  • Created 'commit' which allows trip planners to outline in detail how a plan will go
  • Book: A Path into Sailing
  • Book concept: (A Primer for Makers) Getting to Level 2, how to work with complex tools to build things people want

Things he would like to do more of:

  • Organize communities around environmental sustainable movements
  • Explore coastal waterways
  • Deploy sensors in remote areas
  • Teach sailing and tool building

Groups he's worked with:

  • Anchorage Innovation Team: The SmallBizAlerts program - a communication platform to keep small businesses aware of policy changes and funding resources at the outbreak of COVID-19 (2020)
  • US Digital Response: Organized operations for a distributed group of digital responders who came together to help local governments deal with the burdens the pandemic put on their digital systems (2020)
  • Sequence Environmental: Mapping environmental data along California coastal waterways via drones and PCR to better understand where runoff contamination occurs and spreads (2019)
  • Adventure Report: A monthly adventure storytelling session, Founder; Still running since 2014
  • Boatless Sailing: A group of charter sailors who've been sailing around the world since 2012
  • 3Scan: 1st Employee - Used brain-slicing robots to help automated digital pathology (2013-2018)
  • Sanivation: Co-Founder (2011-2012) - Sanitation as a service
