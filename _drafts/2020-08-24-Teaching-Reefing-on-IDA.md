Kate asked me to walk through reefing on IDA yesterday, and in doing so I found myself learning more about our system than I realized I knew. It's easy to assume you understand a process, but teaching others not only helps them, it helps you.

Teaching sailing on trips with friends has made me a better sailor, passage planner, and communicator, maybe. So: take people out on the water.
