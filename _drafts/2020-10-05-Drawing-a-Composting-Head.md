Converting to a composting head on Petrichor. We have a pseudo-working Jabsco marine head that gets clogged every time we drop solids. The argument for a blackwater holding tank is that you don't have to handle waste directly, whereas with a composting toilet you do.

After three hours with hose clamps, snakes, and leaks working on the head last week, I found I was spending far more time with waste than I ever have with composting. And leaks are about the worst thing aboard.

So the head comes out this week. First sketch is a simple box, starting with the hole patterns and tube outlines.

Also drawing and sketching more day-to-day projects this October. Small consistent steps.
