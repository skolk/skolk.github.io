That's a wrap on one week sailing around SF. Did the Bay Cruiser Triple Crown: Richardson's Bay, Ayala Cove, and Aquatic Park tonight. Up to Drake's and then to Bodega. Lots of motoring, some fun sailing, killed the batteries, and called a tow just before the solar panels recharged.

We got to practice a few different tactics: trysail, reefing underway, lots of surfing and autopilot combinations. Thought we broke the autopilot and the jib furler, fixed both. Still haven't run out of diesel or LPG. Had enough food to only need to stock up on eggs.

Holy hell, I love sleeping bags and down booties. I kind of don't understand why we didn't do boats earlier. There are way fewer camp fees.
