So, I commandeered a drifting sailboat off the Whidbey Island coast. Kayaked out in 3-foot swell, surfing probably 3 miles, got rolled and wet-exited, climbed on board, set sail, and took it to the closest port, single-handed, mainsail only, very wet and cold. Sailed into the harbor where a friend had organized the whole marina to catch me in a dinghy and guide the boat to a mooring. Fun times.

Oh, and the Coast Guard and the owner knew. Just doing a good deed, I'm not ransoming a boat.
