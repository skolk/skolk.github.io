Haven't posted in a while. Kate and I just spent a week sailing from Valencia to Ibiza on a friend's boat, gunkholing with the tourists and finding remote spots out of the way.

Didn't know what to expect from the notorious party island, but it turns out there are hikes, birds, and plenty of quiet spots to watch the sunset, relax, and get some sailing in.

Oh, and I turned 35.
