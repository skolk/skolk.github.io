Dhow sailing in Lamu for the sunset. I talked to the captain for a while about different words in Swahili they use for sailing terms. I have no idea how these are spelled, but a tack is a "yoshi" (which is also the word for the line attached to the sail), a run is a "dengi," and the main sheet being held is the "nmani." Good sunset, good lesson.
