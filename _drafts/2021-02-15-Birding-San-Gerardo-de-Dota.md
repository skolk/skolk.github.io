In our continued efforts to be around wildlife instead of people, Kate and I drove down to San Gerardo de Dota to sleep next to a river. We added ten new birds to the list on the first day. The whole town sits on a river, and the clouds came in at midday, giving a mix of morning sunshine and rolling fog.
