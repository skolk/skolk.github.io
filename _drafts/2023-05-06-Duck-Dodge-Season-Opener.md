Great start to Duck Dodge season. I met several new sailors looking to crew on other boats and find ways to get on the water. Some of them even followed up after the raft-up, which can be hard; social lubrication tends to make memories blurry.

Looking forward to next week, and this weekend, and all spring to progressing at racing skills, meeting co-conspirators, and learning the fine details of trim.
