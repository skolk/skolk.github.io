This past weekend Kate and I drove up to Orcas Island and hiked Mt. Constitution. The San Juans have been a fixture every year in the PNW: we've hiked, kayaked, and sailed around them over the past decade, and they keep pulling us further north. This time we were scouting safe harbors for future cruising and learning more about the layout of the island. We found an artist co-op, a new wren, and met up with the Orcas Island Yacht Club for the Round the County pre-race party. More on the race tomorrow.
