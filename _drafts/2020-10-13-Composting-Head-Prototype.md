Composting prototype continues. Draft, make a bill of materials, use the cheapest parts available to move fast and get dimensions I otherwise wouldn't have.

Most of a composting toilet is urine separation and smell reduction. Everything else supports those two things.

This version is a bit bulky, so version two will be smaller: the diversion unit, the seat, and the frame. But we now have a working model.
