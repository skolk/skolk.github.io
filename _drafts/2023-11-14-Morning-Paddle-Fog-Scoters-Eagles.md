Sometimes you wait for the calms to get out in the vehicle you have. We've been sailing a lot this past year, but paddling in the morning fog feels like a wonderful separation from the wind.

We keep two boards on Petrichor, and they fit easily in the van for land trips.

Out on the water this past weekend: bald eagles, surf scoters, mergansers, long-tailed ducks, belted kingfishers, and plenty of gulls.

Breathe deep.
