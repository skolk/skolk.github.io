Duck Dodge continues to be one of the best events I've come across on the water. It's still a chaotic mess of sailboats trying not to hit paddleboarders, but with three tiers of boats going out, a vague assemblage of opting in, and a great way to meet people afterward on other boats. Also: costumes, but I'm bad at that.

Got to catch up with friends while sailing with a good crew last night. Nowhere close to placing, but great company, and got to connect some people getting into sailing with experienced ones.

Happy times.
