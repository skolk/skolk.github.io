A reflective summer solstice to you. On this longest day of the year, I look back on the highs and lows of the past six months, then forward to the next six with intent, focus, and goals.

This past six was a lot. Kate and I moved to Washington, we sailed the Salish Sea from Victoria and raced in several events with many new sailing friends. I skied the Wapta Range, which had been on my list since we first drove from San Francisco to Jasper back in 2014. I also helped coordinate space robotics groups and an academic workshop next month.

Looking forward to more sailing, mountains, gatherings, and projects over the next six.

If you want to play with this idea, I've left the steps in the next few pictures. And in all seriousness, feel free to reach out if you'd like to talk through where you've been and where you want to go next. I do love a good life-path deconstruction.

Breathe deep.
