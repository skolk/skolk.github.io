Kate and crew took a 22-mile hike along Kauai's north shore on the Kalalau Trail. Rain to match the sun, and rainbows throughout.

Crew of seven, with an accidental pickup of an eighth, hiked in six miles to a mosquito-filled campsite. Half of us then headed out another five miles hoping to get a water taxi back, only to get turned away and hike eleven miles back instead.

Lots of Tropic Birds, Common Mynas, orchids, White-eyed Warblers, and island goats.
