The great thing about summiting with friends is that you've overcome your trials together, and get to look at all the possibilities in front of you from your new vantage. You reflect on your own actions and those of others, step outside yourself, give to the purpose of a team, contribute and be supported.

Glad to have teachers like you in my life. Thanks for introducing me to new facets of the wild. May our paths continue to weave, include, shape, and explore.
