Hotel Cabo Hostel. We arrived mid-day and walked the marina as the sun set, loud bass from the bars, viagra advertisements on the street corners. We found our way to a rodeo and watched dressage horses and local teenagers dance. The morning sun in the hostel was the aftermath.
