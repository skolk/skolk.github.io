After questioning, discussion, testing, and a fair amount of inquiry, Kate and I decided to look for a combination dinghy and lifeboat. We're planning coastal sailing in the near future and needed a dinghy either way, but the question became: hard bottom, RIB (rigid inflatable), fully inflatable, and what are you doing for a life raft if you go offshore?

The Portland Pudgy is a multi-tool across all those options. Not the best at any one, but it can motor, sail, row, and serve as a life raft. It's not the lightest option, but it hauls up, and, as I found out, fits in the van.

I drove down to San Diego to pick up the only used one on the West Coast. (Slept in the van on the way down, then not so much once the yellow beast was inside.) They have quite the cult following, which is always something to examine.

We'll update on how the next month goes, as we get our two vessels ready to head south.
