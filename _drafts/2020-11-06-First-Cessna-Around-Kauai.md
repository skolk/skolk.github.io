First Cessna flight, around Kauai with one of Kate's coworkers piloting. It wasn't nearly as turbulent as we'd braced for, thinking of the coastal thermals in Kenya. Amazing lighting. The two-day, 22-mile Napali Coast hike became a three-minute flyover.

Our guide pointed out coffee fields, solar installations, swell breaks, and gave a geographic history of the island you'd normally only get in a slide deck.
