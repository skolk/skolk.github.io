When one project turns into another. Starting version two of the composting head, I needed to descale the heat exchanger first, clearing out calcium buildup on the inside. So I hooked up a small pot with 12VDC and am circulating barnacle buster through it. It starts blue and goes brown. Running it for at least an hour.

Probably need more buckets on this boat.
