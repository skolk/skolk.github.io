Three of us hiked out and climbed the Crescent Couloir on Round Top. Prepared to ski out, but the morning pack was hard, brittle, and perfect for a steep snow ascent. We ended up climbing unroped about 500 feet, rapped one pitch on a natural snow anchor, and down-climbed the rest. Winds were 30 to 40 at the crest; the couloir protected us, but that was enough for the day.

Ski and sail on a Saturday. Ended up at Clipper Cove after.
