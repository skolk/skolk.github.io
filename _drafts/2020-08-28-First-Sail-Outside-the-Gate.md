Sailing outside the Gate for the first time, rounding out and around rather than just to Point Bonita. We ended up motoring the way down to Half Moon Bay, but getting into the swell and watching the bridge fade behind us was a context shift worth thinking about.

Before this trip we made sure to have an EPIRB, safety lines, a dinghy in case of anything catastrophic, and plenty of water. Small steps toward larger goals.
