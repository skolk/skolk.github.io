Spinner dolphins off the coast of Kauai. Kate and I took a day to dive with a local dive operation. No underwater shots, but on the way out this pod swam with us for about fifteen minutes.

We also crossed paths with a less friendly pod of bottlenose dolphins, plenty of Brown Boobies, and a Green Sea Turtle before going under. The current was strong enough that each dive site turned into three, so we got six sites across two dives.

I love caves and turtles. Much joy. Now to tacos.
