New port. Rounding Drakes Bay out to Point Reyes and then up to Bodega Bay was all motoring, but a good time with big swell and crab traps to avoid. Once we parked Petrichor we went for a hike, watched the sunset, and made dinner.

It's a lot like RVs, or tiny houses, but it floats. And you need a safety line, and we're learning to handle larger swell and to plan passages around different wind and swell patterns. We were supposed to head to LA this week, but that doesn't seem to be in the lineup for this year.

All of our ski gear is now boat gear. Getting ready for a blowy day feels a lot like getting ready for alpine touring or mountaineering.
