Happy May. I started exploring different AI prototyping tools this past weekend and realized I enjoy the paradox of choice when you can "make" anything.

I've had a similar experience with reading: when a bookstore is no longer your boundary, what do you read? So as I prompted the infinity machine to imagine ideas, I began exploring the types of growth and movements I'm excited to see: problems solved in novel ways, by those who might not have been given the tools to solve them. If you want to change the world, teach someone how to use a new tool.

So this May Day, I made a series of ideas I found inspiring. Here's to the hope that you're working on something that fulfills you.
