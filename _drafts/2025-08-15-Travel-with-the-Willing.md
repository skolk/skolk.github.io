In deciding paths and travel companions, it's easiest to find those already traveling in the same direction as you. Travel with the willing. Attempting a trail with a partner going somewhere else doesn't work well. Disagreeing even half the time is wearing, and moving at an agreeable pace with an agreeable agenda makes for much easier travel.

This past month we found co-conspirators: new friends who were already on the path we'd set out for, whose previous decisions led us to the same place, at the same time, with similar projects, problems, and challenges on a boat, in a van, and on the snow.

Here's to the co-conspirators we've met, and those we'll meet in the upcoming journeys. Here's to the hope that you find yours.
