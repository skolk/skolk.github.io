First full day working out of the van, 9 to 5. Power bank and all. The bench folds down so I can also lie down and work. I can close the door, sleep if I want to, spread out, lock up, and drive out if needed. About 8 to 10 hours of battery and cell signal.

Definitely some improvements to come, mostly in storage, maybe a way to rotate the front seats, but it's a working prototype.
