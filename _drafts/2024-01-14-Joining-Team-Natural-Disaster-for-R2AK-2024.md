It's official. I'll be joining Team Natural Disaster for Race to Alaska this June. Washington to Ketchikan, Alaska, 850 miles, no motor.

We'll be taking Team Monkey Fist's Olson 30, Wildfire, up the coast, pushing ourselves to learn more about the Pacific Northwest coast and the waters around her islands. Going to be a cold, wet, wonderful summer.
