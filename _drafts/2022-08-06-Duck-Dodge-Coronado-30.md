Getting out on Lake Union with Duck Dodge, meeting new sailors, learning a new city, and finding out more things I don't know about boats.

This round I went out on a Coronado 30 with a single-cylinder diesel and a full kite. Many crashes averted. We didn't place, but it was a great way to meet people and learn more than you get from just rafting up afterward.
