Learning by doing. We dropped anchor in China Cove on Angel Island knowing it was a rough bottom. When Kate and Kai pulled up the anchor the next morning, no windlass, we rigged a haul system using a halyard and found our CQR had snagged another chain on the way up. It took a small operation to get it free. We might have come away with a bonus anchor, but we'd hit our drama limit for the day (it was noon by the time we were free), so we dropped the chain and sailed on to China Camp, then Richardson Bay.
