Not a great artist, but I'm going to keep trying. Sketching allows me to focus and shuts off the internal monologue that needs some rest at times.

The most useful tool I've been exposed to for sailing in the past year is the soft shackle. The one I've been wearing as a bracelet while sailing was given to me around Ibiza. I've used them for foresail attachments and love having both carabiners and shackles for foredeck work.

Time to learn to make them and get the correct fids.

See you on the water.
