I've been spending more time on the foredeck over the past two years, learning kite lines and how they tend to cross and snag. It's a mental game that's humbled me.

Kate got this picture last week after a hourglass set, and the helmsman reminded me to do things slowly the right way, not fast the wrong way. So now I get to repack all the kites on the boat, set up the lines, and recreate the errors I've run into to learn how to fix them. Step by step.
