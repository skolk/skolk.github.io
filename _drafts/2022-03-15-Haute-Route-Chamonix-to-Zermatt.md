We just skied the Haute Route, Chamonix to Zermatt via Verbier: four huts, one hotel, a few ski lifts, and lots of skinning and crusty descents. A classic route through the Alps, with many other parties along the way, historic peaks and runs, and more glaciers than I can count. We navigated back up over crevasses more than once.

It was different with a guide, but it also let us push physically harder without the stress of reading unfamiliar terrain.
