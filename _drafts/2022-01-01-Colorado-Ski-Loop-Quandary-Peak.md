Should probably post something from the past two months. We made it to Cabo, then up to La Paz, and then Loreto by sail. But first: I spent last week in Boulder skiing around the Rockies. First at Tundra Hut, then around Frisco. Thought I'd get in-bounds at some point, but apparently I'd rather torture my feet.

Yesterday I soloed 2,000 feet up Quandary Peak, then did another 1,000 with a friend at Mayflower Gulch. Now I'm exhausted and need a recovery day.

Out of shape. Need to hike more.
