Happy to have raced Petrichor for the first time, even if we didn't make the halfway mark in time. We got to fly a kite, dance around another competitor, and get passed by SV Blue Jeans (and every other boat out there). Learning more about current and how to avoid traps. It was a light wind weekend, so none of this is a surprise for our heavy girl.

At the party in Port Townsend we got to meet other cruisers and racers from all over the Salish Sea, and got recommendations on day sails and spots up north for cruises later this summer.

Now to track down all the names of the crew I only remember by vessel name.
