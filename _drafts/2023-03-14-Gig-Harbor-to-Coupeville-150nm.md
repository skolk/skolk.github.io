Got a good 150 nautical miles in with two friends last weekend, exploring Gig Harbor up to Coupeville, with humpbacks, surf scoters, grebes, and lots of guillemots along the way.

I finally broke down and got a real set of foulies. The mountaineering Gore-Tex wasn't cutting it anymore: it just seeps, and it wasn't holding up. Need to rethink the boots too. I have a wetsuit I can always pull on, but it's hard to take off to pee.

It was only in the 40s, but a longer passage overall than our Victoria crossing.

More to explore gear-wise later. Right now I'm just windkissed and proud of my skipper and her crew for pulling off a whole weekend in 25-knot winds with everyone docking in good spirits.

Now off to plan the next trips.
