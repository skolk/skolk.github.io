What I enjoy about touring is what I love about alpine scrambling. It's not the fresh powder, or even the solitude. It's the environmental and interpersonal challenges you throw yourself into, to play with your perceptions, desires, skill levels, and growth potential. There is no win. There is no best day. There is only learning and presence.

Be well.
