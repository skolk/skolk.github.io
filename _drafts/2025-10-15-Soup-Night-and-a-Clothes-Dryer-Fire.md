Along the way, we keep finding small groups of people who share their fires. If it were purely transactional, you'd pay. Instead it's contributive: you bring and share and contribute.

These small gatherings keep pulling new people into our lives, and inspire us to do the same.

This particular fire was in a reclaimed clothes dryer, part of a stone-soup-concept soup night that resulted in a lot of boat talk.

We're back in Seattle. If you're around for a fire this equinox, give or take a week, come find us.
