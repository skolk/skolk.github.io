Thanks for all the birthday wishes, they were received up high. Kate and I were planning to climb Sahale this past weekend, but bear presence on the route had us divert to Shuksan via the Sulfide Glacier, mixing the gully and the ridge on the upper mountain.

Amazing views at the top, with the northern ranges on the horizon and Baker looming overhead. The crevasses were mostly filled in. Temperatures at 2 am in the 50s, the warmest I've ever spent on a glacier. This was the first time we used our lighter glacier kit: trail shoes with neoprene socks, 6-millimeter line, 30-liter pack. Everything went smoothly, with a few minor changes we'll make before next week.
