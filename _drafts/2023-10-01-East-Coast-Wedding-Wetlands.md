Earlier this month I spent some time on the East Coast for wedding number three this fall. I hadn't been back in a while, and it was a good reminder of how much fried chicken exists down there, that coffee can cost less than $5, and that there are remarkable wetlands everywhere.

This was the start of three weeks of work travel for me. Kate and I took some time off to see herons, egrets, friends who live in Kenya, and a newly married couple.
