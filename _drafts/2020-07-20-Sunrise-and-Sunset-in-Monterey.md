Working an odd gig out here from my past life as a hardware engineer. I'd forgotten how much grease gets everywhere.

It's the longest I've been away from Kate since the pandemic started, and it's practically car camping: all my own food, water, and wifi. I haven't been closer than ten feet to anyone in Monterey. I get to see the coast on morning runs, alone. Totally fine with van coffee and a morning office, time to think and write until I go work for the robots.
