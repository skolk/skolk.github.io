A world so different from your comfort zone. On the wildlife count today: elk, California Condor, Canyon Wren, Javelina, and Gooseberry. Learned which shrubs the Hopi use to weave baskets. Saw the emerald green river and the wind-blown cedar.

There were struggles too: small arguments, bloody noses in the dry air, elk less than happy about the distance we gave them, and a copperhead that was genuinely close.

But the big and small dance their dance, and always have. If you stop, listen, look, and wait, the life peaks out.
