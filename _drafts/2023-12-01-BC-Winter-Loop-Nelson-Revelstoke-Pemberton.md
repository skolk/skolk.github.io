Kate and I completed our BC winter loop over the break: stops in Nelson, Revelstoke, and Pemberton.

Low snowpack and "sharky" conditions, but we made the best of it. In Nelson, we skied slack country off Whitewater after attending the local snowpack briefing, a great format: guides explaining current conditions, updated routes, recent developments, and safety notes, all while gathering the tribe in one place. We really enjoyed the Nelson area, its variety of terrain, and the lakes with sailboats on them.

Christmas in Nelson: yoga, cocktails, and locally roasted coffee in the morning. We also got an update on how to use FatMap and CalTopo to find huts, terrain, and local routes. Happy to have new tools in the bag.
