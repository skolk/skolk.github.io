And we're off. It's been a great spring in Seattle getting ready for the race and the cruise north. We were planning to race to Alaska and send Petrichor up before us, but schedules got swapped. Now I'm off at 4 am with crew aboard, headed for Port Hardy first and hopefully Ketchikan after. This means moving our home and exploring the north before coming back down and racing it all over again without a motor.

80-plus miles a day in coastal water for two weeks.

See you in the fall, Shilshole.
