We bought a 1990 Catalina 36 and took it out for our first loop around the bay. Friday we were in Clipper Cove; Saturday we rounded Angel Island and anchored in China Cove, one cove over from Ayala. No idea where we'll be tonight.

Lots of work ahead: lines to tend, a depth sounder to replace, a bimini to sew. Hoping to make it out to Bodega Bay before the solstice, and maybe further down the coast by winter.
