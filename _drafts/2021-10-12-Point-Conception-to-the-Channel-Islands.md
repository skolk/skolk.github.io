Made it around Point Conception and down to the islands. It was about 90 nautical miles from Avila to Santa Rosa, where we tucked into Becher's Bay for the night with 10 to 15 knots of wind but a solid swell break. Lots of reading, swifts, gulls, and dolphins along the way, and all the wind beat into us from some awkward directions.

I also trust my new G40 chain, and have lived to tell about it. Now to fix that anchor light.
