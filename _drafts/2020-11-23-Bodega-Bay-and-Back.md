The many phases of predawn departures. Kate and I made it up to Bodega, and after a day of bird watching, set off back south in 2-meter swell and 10 degree weather. Not as much wind as we hoped, but 50 nautical miles back down to SF Bay. Lots of sun and reading, talking about what design is and isn't, and learning to tell different gulls apart.

More sails, less motor tomorrow.

Also: we made plans for the next three months, so that'll change a few things.
