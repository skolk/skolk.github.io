San Simeon to Avila. Calm day, only eight hours of motoring. Getting odd about thinking of eight hours as a short travel day. Again less fog.

Stepped on land to find a power outage, so both the diesel and grocers were closed. Had a fun surf landing and a short hike, then decided land is overrated. Then a more exciting surf entry, wine, otters, bed.

Off for another long day to the Channel Islands tomorrow.
