A new country, and one with trains and walkable streets. It's already been both overwhelming and wonderful. I don't remember seeing so many business-formal people since traveling through New York, and I'd been told it would happen, but it's obvious how much I stand over and out of most people here.

I'm amazed by the public transportation, the ease of walking, and the number of small restaurants, cozy holes in the wall that seem to be surviving. No idea what the next four days will bring. This feels like the first time I've traveled in a while, which is a complicated statement.
