For three of the early mornings on the way down, this was most of what I was looking at, plus Navionics on my phone, but I was taking the picture. Too much fog to see more than two boat lengths ahead, so between GPS, chart, and eyesight, we couldn't reliably see unmarked buoys or crab traps. We slowed down, but radar really helped.
