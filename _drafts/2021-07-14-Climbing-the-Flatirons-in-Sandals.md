First sandal solo. Not sure I recommend it.

Woke up in Boulder without climbing shoes, so a friend and I climbed the Second Flatiron (Freeway, 4th class / 5.0) in about an hour, me in Luna sandals. A few sketchy moments, mostly from my sweaty feet slipping with ankle-rolling potential. I think it was 800 feet. We then walked Pearl Street and I bought new approach shoes.

Good for a Saturday before noon.
