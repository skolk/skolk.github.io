I'll finish the R2AK posts in a bit, but excited about this morning's expedition to Le Conte Glacier. We navigated bergy bits and didn't scratch the boat. The blue ice and calving face were creaking through the wind, a reminder of how chaotic this area can get. We could only get so close in fiberglass, and there will be more ice up north.

Aside: we'll be somewhat remote for the next week. I know people will try to reach me. Thank you in advance; might be a while before I get back to you.
