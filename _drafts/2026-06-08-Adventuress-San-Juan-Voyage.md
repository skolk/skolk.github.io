For World Ocean Day, I'm announcing a collaboration with Sound Experience: a four-day voyage aboard the schooner Adventuress for those of us living, working, and exploring the waters of the Salish Sea. We leave Port Townsend and explore the San Juans, September 17th through 20th, following the Port Townsend Wooden Boat Festival.

It was inspired by the voyage Maritime Blue coordinated last October from Seattle to San Francisco, which showed what focused time together with the right people can shift: your sense of what's possible, and how a crew comes to work as one.

This gathering of the creative, curious, generative, and resourceful is meant to let us exchange and grow from our time together. New skills, new perspectives, new ways of being.

Applications to join are open at [islandlab.dev/sanjuan26](https://islandlab.dev/sanjuan26). Looking forward to seeing you out there.
