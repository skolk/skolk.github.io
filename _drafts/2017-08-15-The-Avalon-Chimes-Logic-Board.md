Walking past the Avalon Chimes, we saw the gate open. Charlie invited us in and showed us how the whole system worked, and let us play a few notes.

It is a series of step-up solenoids, hand-wound from 1915, with a mechanical governor and a rotating timing drum. The striking solenoid hits one of 20 chimes. Charlie has been maintaining the system for over 20 years and working to improve the space. He then showed us his workshop in Avalon and lent us a skiff for the night.
