We're uprooting from West Oakland for a bit, moving out to spend time in the Pacific and take lengthy remote calls from one of the more remote islands there is. (It looks like a vacation, but I've been up between 2am and 5am every day since Saturday to call New York or London.)

All of these shots were taken walking from the new base. The Shearwater had been at a shelter for a few days and was being released, the caregivers watching two Shearwaters and a Storm Petrel take off from the cliffs.

The turtles have been coming back in scores: seven on the beach in a single evening. Much life. Many new life-list entries.
