Yesterday, Thursday, after a great start out of Victoria, we found ourselves on the outside of Active Pass in the Strait of Georgia with twilight on the water. Then: a loud pop and a crash, and we were without a forestay, in emergency mode to keep the mast standing. We secured a halyard and pedaled toward land.

Tomorrow's update will walk through the path to the next leg of the race.
