At least once a month I try to get out for an overnight, sunset to sunrise: sailing, bike camping, or fast-packing one night. This time, a midweek sail left from Berkeley Marina, crossed the Bay to Treasure Island, and anchored in Clipper Cove.

Kate had planned that it was easier to get to work from the island than to sail back, so she took a 6am plunge with a dry bag and called a ride from shore before heading to Palo Alto. The rest of us took a placid motor back to the marina to start the work day.
