After the Baja Ha-Ha dropped me into Cabo at the end of 2021, *Petrichor* and I spent the next several months working north into the Sea of Cortez and back again. Anchorages in Bahia de los Muertos, Isla Espiritu Santo, La Paz, Loreto. Long stretches at anchor between short passages.

The capstone was double-handing the boat engineless from Loreto down to La Paz, anchoring entirely under sail. The Cortez teaches self-reliance the way few places do, the nearest chandlery is a long way off when your impeller goes, and the wildlife is worth every mile of getting there.

Stub for now. The full season is still to be written.
