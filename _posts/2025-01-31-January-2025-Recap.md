January opened on [Petrichor](/sailing) at Shilshole with Kate and a long list of intentions. By month-end Kate had left for the Southern Atlantic on *Amundsen* with Skip Novak, My father-in-law had passed, and the year's projects were named for the first time.

**Family.** Jan 6, after a day at the hospital with Kate and her family, My father-in-law passed. I wrote two pieces about him in the weeks after: a long eulogy ("Song of Roland") about kayaking in Blackfish Sound, and a sharper one about the Philips HR irony that had shaped his last years. Grief and momentum are not opposites. Not getting off the boat isn't something to brag about; it's the modality to fight.

## Projects

**Astraeus.** Multiple syncs with Alex Parker and Tanya Harrison through the month. Jan 8 mapped the funding picture (NOAA SBIR, Schmidt, Seaworthy Collective, Maritime Blue's 100K for 6 percent). Jan 17 the strategy talk that named the obvious: acquisition is more realistic than IPO. Jan 22 we worked through TAM and the derelict ghost-gear angle for Puget Sound tribes alongside Gulf oil and gas. Jan 30 Brock Mansfield and Jean-noel Poirier at Maritime Blue for the pitch.

**REAP.** Four-hour blocks on grants with Jonathan DeLong. Firedoll and Sam's Foundation submissions out the door. The frame: submit 2 grants, 2 contract proposals, finalize 2 pitches. Get the deck done.

**SASD.** The LLC, the taxes, the website. Rate sketched at $100 to $150 an hour.

**Nahlia.** Jayant Menon's wearable BMI work for DOD and DHA. Two calls. A contract drafted at $4,500 a month, 40 hours.

**Mountains and water.** Source Lake with Davin on Jan 2 to open the year. Backcountry day with Ajay, Connor, Payam, and Nick around Jan 27. Dinner aboard Mark and Chelsea's Bayliner *Muff* on Jan 29.

**The boat.** 3D printer up. Diesel heater testing. Second LiPo planned. Shelves, propane locker, 120V outlet by my side of the bed.

**Finds.** David Lang on Jan 18: don't be in a rush to buy the suit. Meggy: if you're doing anything with VC, take equity. Both lines stuck.
