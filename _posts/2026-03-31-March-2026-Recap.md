Lots of travel again this month — Alaska for a shellfish conference, driving down to San Francisco by way of Portland and back up again via Eureka, and taking *Petrichor* out to the San Juans.

## Travel

- **[Chumstock](https://www.chumstock.com/)**: my friend Aaron put together an incredible event with 120 people and the right ratios of gender identities, thinkers vs. doers, ages, and time split between structured, unstructured, and one-to-many vs. many-to-many. It was amazing — I need to write more on it.
- **Anchorage**: attended the [Alaska Mariculture Conference](https://alaskaseagrant.org/events/2026-mariculture-conference-of-alaska/) and was amazed at how cold the city can be. Met so many oyster and kelp growers; fascinated by all the work they do.
- **Portland**: attended the [National Shellfish Association](https://www.shellfish.org/) conference.
- **Berkeley**: four days at [Bioneers](https://bioneers.org/) with many friends, and saw lots of old friends and family.
- **San Francisco**: always good to stop in.
