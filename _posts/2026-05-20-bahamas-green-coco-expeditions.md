A week off Exuma with [Green Coco Expeditions](https://greencoco.org/) aboard the 60-foot catamaran *Nesi*. The route ran through Hog Cut to Stone Cay, Water Cay, and Flamingo Key.

Blue holes, loggerhead turtles, reef sharks, jackfish, dives past ten meters. Jana, Sabrina, and Kristian ran the trip with real generosity, always lowering the barrier to get people into the water. Best charter I've been on. I want to bring that energy to our own Boatless Sailing trips.
