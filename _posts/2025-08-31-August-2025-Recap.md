## Church Mountain

Aug 8 solo summit out of Glacier. I woke up at 5am and decided to go. The cables section. A fall: palms and cheek slammed into the rock, wind knocked out of me. Met John training for Rim-to-Rim. Burrito at Wake and Bake. Then sailed Petrichor into the San Juans with Kate for an anchor-out sunset dinner. The line that came out of the slip on the ridge: when we lose focus, touch, connection with our environment, surroundings, presence, when we are multitasking or taking shortcuts, that is when we slip.

## Climate weekend

A series of conversations mid-month at a resiliency potluck. The thread that became the long piece on the ideological superstructure: Marxist superstructure as emergent property, the American Dream as a banker-rewarded mesh.

## Lines

Of course you're a connector, but connecting the dots isn't building the bridge, and building the bridge isn't moving the goods, and moving the goods isn't setting up shop to feed people. Whimsy: what is, is, let us play with it. Mudge: no, it should be this way, make it different. Let's get real or let's not play. Your goal is now to solve people's problems for dollars, and gain optionality tokens, and not have to spend your own.
