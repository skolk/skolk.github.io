Hello Friends and family!

Catching up:

It’s been quite the year, in-fact, it’s been quite the year and a half. As most of you know. Kate end I left San Francisco last Summer and spent the following period in Europe and Africa. I’ll cut it off at the start of 2019, but to keep it short: We climbed the [Glaciated Mt Stanley](https://www.instagram.com/p/BsCklpZnUun/), the High point of Uganda and the DRC, saw [Chimpanzees](https://www.instagram.com/p/BsPW4mkHBKu/) in the Central Africa Rainforest, [Climbed Mt. Kenya](https://www.instagram.com/p/Btd4U75nUaL/) the 20 Pitch 5.8 at 17,000 ft (the 2nd highest point in Africa), I attended the online [atlMBA](https://altmba.com/), Attended[Afrika Burn](https://www.afrikaburn.com/) as a construction hand on [Octocrab](https://www.google.com/search?bih=697&biw=1420&dpr=2&q=Octo%20Crab%20afrikaburn&sa=X&spell=1&sxsrf=ACYBGNSZIaTiaJGzQbzufu8FmDe1oEZcPA%3A1569259517571&tbm=isch&ved=0ahUKEwipnOugu-fkAhXiITQIHSUsBkYQBQg6KAA), and became a Africa Burn Ranger; helping burners around the city and providing a safe burn perimeter during the burn events.

Kate and I then; Spent time in [Mauritius](https://www.instagram.com/p/BxWT2C2HsWj/), went sailing in [Greece](https://www.instagram.com/p/ByigvTHH6OY/) for two weeks, spent a month back in the US, went back to Kenya, packed up the house, sold everything, and went hiking around [Scotland](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0yuWwIn3WW/) and Manchester area for two weeks before flying back to the US.  I went out to Burning Man; where he passed his [Black Rock Ranger](https://rangers.burningman.org/) alpha and assisted as a green dot ranger (helping those who are in overwhelmed stated) and  camped with [Burners Without Boarders](https://www.burnerswithoutborders.org/) as co-build Lead of their 100 person camp.

The Now:

well… now I’m back in San Francisco with Kate, where she’s leading [IDEO’s](https://www.ideo.com/) build of the Digital Dream Lab, and we look for housing in San Francisco, and my next career.

We’re staying with friends around the San Francisco, and potentially Oakland area. House hunting in SF isn’t all that fun, and I don’t recommend it; especially when one of you has a job! It’s been stressful to be honest, and we might find a less than ideal situation to allow me to focus on Job searches, and work that comes up in between.

Job wise, I’m looking for a way to work with a group that’s facing challenging problems win the environmental and generative space; Ocean Tech is attractive at the moment, but I’ve also been looking at energy companies and groups that are making hardware tools to better solve problems that cause industrial waste and affect how we consume and generate. For the past half decade I’ve learned how to lead hardware engineering projects; with mechanical systems and electronic development. I see myself as understanding how to build and would like to play in the human engineering side working with teams and understanding problems spaces, rather than only building on the engineering side.

I know that’s broad: I want to help people solve problems with systems, automation, and teams. I’m job searching. If you think my background (resume here) would be attractive to a group or person you know, I’d love to be in touch.
