## Travel

**Salt Spring weekends.** Back at friends' on Salt Spring by month-end. Cafe Aroma. Cold plunges. Skipping the social-media noise.

**Swiftsure International.** Around May 24-25 out of Victoria. Golux's biggest shakedown before Van Isle. Highs: understanding how main-sail trim needs to look, finally understanding inside vs outside gybes on the asymmetrical spinnaker, the sunset, the sunrise. Lows: cold legs again, despite all the clothes.

## Projects

**Astraeus.** At least an hour of admin a day. Customer-discovery interview script written around the hypothesis: ocean researchers spend over half their time wrangling data.

**REAP.** Cooling. May 12: I'm not getting a hell yes with REAP. I'm getting a "this isn't the boat I want to be on right now, but I also need to be paid for the work I'm doing." Drafting the MOU and budget to step away cleanly.

**Quantum Alliance and Ujjwal Kumar (HKS).** May 5, May 6, May 15 working sessions on "green growth" framing. Scoping down to forestry. A possible July Boston fundraising trip on the calendar.

**PNDC / Where Sea Meets Sky.** Co-organizing the Maritime and Space-Tech showcase at the Mission Acceleration Center with Dradin, Denise, Andrea, Nigel.

**Petrichor.** Battery box installed May 3. Alternator re-routed. Starter battery switch planned.

**Finds.** Holm captain of Golux. Vulnerable feedback note written to him. Beth the anti-entropic organizer of kites and crew welfare. Kristin, asked for her mental models on team dynamics. Jake and Molly. Molly off to sail the Great Lakes. Tina, asking how to be vulnerable. Kavi Reddy on AI, soil-food, permaculture training (May 8). **Published.** May 27 from Salt Spring: ["Finding Your Gaps and Working with Teams: Lessons from the Cockpit"](/2025/05/27/Finding-Your-Gaps.html). First real ship of the year.

**Lines.** Security means owning the process, and knowing that you don't need more all the time.

**Looking ahead.** 30 days to plan solstice plus. Van Isle 360 in June. Submit RII. Submit a Quantum alliance grant. Roam the Island for 20 days between race legs. Six-Pack captain's license. RYA sailing school. Dinner with Beth and Holm in June.
