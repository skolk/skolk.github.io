## Travel

**Salt Spring weekends.** Back at friends' on Salt Spring by month-end. Cafe Aroma. Cold plunges. Skipping the social-media noise.

**Swiftsure International.** Around May 24-25 out of Victoria. Golux's biggest shakedown before Van Isle. Highs: understanding how main-sail trim needs to look, finally understanding inside vs outside gybes on the asymmetrical spinnaker, the sunset, the sunrise. Lows: cold legs again, despite all the clothes.

## Projects

**Astraeus.** At least an hour of admin a day. Customer-discovery interview script written around the hypothesis: ocean researchers spend over half their time wrangling data.

**Climate nonprofit work.** Cooling. May 12: I'm not getting a hell yes with this work. I'm getting a "this isn't the boat I want to be on right now, but I also need to be paid for the work I'm doing." Drafting the MOU and budget to step away cleanly.

**Quantum Alliance and Ujjwal Kumar (HKS).** May 5, May 6, May 15 working sessions on "green growth" framing. Scoping down to forestry. A possible July Boston fundraising trip on the calendar.

**PNDC / Where Sea Meets Sky.** Co-organizing the Maritime and Space-Tech showcase at the Mission Acceleration Center with Dradin, Denise, Andrea, Nigel.

**Petrichor.** Battery box installed May 3. Alternator re-routed. Starter battery switch planned.

**Finds.** The captain of Golux, with a vulnerable feedback note written to him. A crewmate, the anti-entropic organizer of kites and crew welfare. Another crewmate I asked for mental models on team dynamics. A friend on AI, soil-food, permaculture training (May 8). A friend at U Iowa on biosensors: many smaller contracts beat one large one. A friend playing with warehouses and real estate. The North Salish conservation contact I cold-emailed for coffee on Quadra.

**Published.** May 27 from Salt Spring: ["Finding Your Gaps and Working with Teams: Lessons from the Cockpit"](/2025/05/27/Finding-Your-Gaps.html). First real ship of the year.

**Lines.** Security means owning the process, and knowing that you don't need more all the time.

**Looking ahead.** 30 days to plan solstice plus. Van Isle 360 in June. Submit RII. Submit a Quantum alliance grant. Roam the Island for 20 days between race legs. Six-Pack captain's license. RYA sailing school. Dinner with the Golux crew in June.
