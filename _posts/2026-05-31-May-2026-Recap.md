The travel-heavy run kept going: New Bedford and Woods Hole with VentureWell, Boston and the Harvard museum, free diving the Bahamas with Green Coco aboard *SV Nesi*, a family event in Toronto, and out to the Inner Hebrides for an islandness gathering on the Isle of Ulva. It opened with sunset sails on *Petrichor*.

## Travel

- **New Bedford / Woods Hole**: down to the old whaling port with [VentureWell](https://venturewell.org/), then over to the [Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution](https://www.whoi.edu/). Standing in the country's deepest fishing-and-oceanography history right where the science and the working waterfront meet.
- **Boston / Cambridge**: the [Harvard Museum of Natural History](https://hmnh.harvard.edu/) got me reading about the explorers and idea-people: [Robert Peary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Peary) to the North Pole, [George Peabody](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Peabody) seeding the anthropology conversations and [Darwin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Darwin)'s contributions. A reminder that exploration is something you get to be part of. Stayed with a friend in Cambridge, "I can build apps that build apps; hardware might be more valuable than software now."
- **Bahamas**: a week off Exuma with [Green Coco Expeditions](https://greencoco.org/) on the 60-foot catamaran *Nesi*; through Hog Cut to Stone Cay, Water Cay, and Flamingo Key. Blue holes, loggerhead turtles, sharks, jackfish, dives past 10m. Best charter I've been on! Jana, Sabrina, and Kristian made it generous and easy, always lowering the barrier to get people in the water. Want to bring that to our own trips.
- **Toronto**: family time with Kate, my mother-in-law, and my uncle.
- **Scotland (Glasgow → Oban → Ulva)**: [Hannah Close](https://hannahlclose.com/)'s Islandness gathering on Ulva, part-residency, part-shoreside philosophy salon. Talked with community members about the land buyout, deer and venison, salmon farms, and the hard governance work that comes after you buy an island. Exactly the coastal-community work we want to be building tools for.

## Finds

- [Hannah Close](https://hannahlclose.com/) and [Simone Weil](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simone_Weil)'s *metaxu*, the in-between. "What if the ocean is the place and the island is the meanwhile."
- The [Dark Mountain Project](https://dark-mountain.net/) and the Uncivilisation manifesto.
- Scottish community land reform: Ulva and Eigg buyouts, Right to Buy, Ostrom's commons in the wild.
- [SAMS](https://www.sams.ac.uk/) at Dunstaffnage and the [Challenger expedition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Challenger_expedition): where modern oceanography starts.
- Nora Bateson and [Warm Data](https://www.warmdata.life/).
- [The Ferret](https://theferret.scot/)'s long investigation into the Ulva buyout, eight years in.
