## Travel

**Braid Theory Ocean Enterprise Accelerator.** Apr 22-25 in Providence. Full day of pitches alongside FishLAT, Octopulse, Drift Marine Systems, Coastal Measures, Sea Gal, Alof autonomous sail rigs, BlueMovement, Lacuum, Hydranet, Blue Sea Farms, Cargo Check. IOOS-funded. The goal: 15 new customer interviews. Got most of them.

**Michigan.** Apr 26-28 with Thomas and Jenna. Thomas drilled me on shipping: n8n, Windsurf, Lovable, Replicate, v0, Render, Slidebean, Forecastr. I built a small app with him. The week's verb was ship.

## Projects

**Astraeus.** A pitch-deck overhaul that opened with the line "intelligence layer behind the majority of real-time, actionable ocean data globally." Hinrik's coaching call was sharp: if I were an investor I would ask why I'm not talking to the CEO. He named the tension with Alex, founder-market fit, the need to pick one specific fish, and the size of the round ($10M+). Beachheads listed: oil/gas spill reduction, USCG, Navy, conservation, fishing, coastal surveillance. Inga Wise on Apr 16 opened up Iceland (IFOMC, Ocean Mind, safenet), CSIRO Australia, MOTT Galveston, the TNC tuna pledge, and Pew.

**REAP.** AI roadmap, water proposal, CRM build in Attio. Hunting $600K in grants and $2 to $4M in private donors. Frustration with Jonathan growing. KPI honesty: $4K a month is OK, real goal is $6K, really $7K (~$80K a year).

**PNW Climate Week (Maritime track).** Planning calls with Nigel Sharp, Michael Town, Zohar Ehrlich. With Bjorn (Maritime Blue is in a funding crisis and can't sponsor a space; May 29 Blue Ventures showcase is on). With Sean Esterly and Mallory Hauser at Port of Seattle on sustainable fuels (Sandy Kilroy follow-up). I started asking the obvious question: why not just host at Maritime Blue?

**SASD.** Find a client to charge $10K a month.

## Quantum Alliance dinner

Apr 28. Ujjwal and Ulpanika. Brainstormed the next-generation prize structure: governments paying for access, dispersing innovation outside the US. The thread that becomes a real project in July.

## Writing

Ship became the verb of the month. Personal site, Salt and Snow blog, the short book of sailing to Alaska and R2AK, the work-I-should-be-doing list. None shipped yet. All were named.
