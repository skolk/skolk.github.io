A transition month. There was a false spring, then a harsh push back into winter. I've been taking *Petrichor* out as much as I could — sunset sails, overnights in Port Ludlow and Port Orchard with Sloop Tavern Yacht Club. Many projects around the water, and preparation for summer travels and Kate in the northern hemisphere.

## Projects

- **RYA Yachtmaster training** — many hours looking over charts and memorizing light patterns for ships in the night.
- **RYA Radio Course** — the requirements of radio and GNSS communication at sea.
- **Advanced First Aid for Sailors** — re-certifying. It's been over two years since the last one.
- **Modeling** — learning ways to map, project, and emulate the world.
- **Biological Digital Twin** — predicting the nominal and off-nominal state of biological objects.
- **Ecosystem mapping (ecological-economic)** — making progress on a framework to map coastlines and their environmental conditions.
- **Community sense-making** — the work above is all in service of groups finding ways to come together to make decisions.
- **Warm Data event** — hosted one.
