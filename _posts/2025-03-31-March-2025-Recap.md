## Projects

**Astraeus.** New deck around "Ocean Intelligence for Maritime Resources Security." The beachhead became fish stocks. The frame became an intel group selling data products to fisheries, regulators (NOAA, USCG), and NGOs. Pitch coaching from a friend (if you're not 10x, you're not VC) and Vincent Nguyen on maritime domain awareness, IUU fishing, Skylight, Starboard.nz. MOUs in progress with American Seafood, Trident, and a ghost-fishing group.

**Climate nonprofit work.** One-pager out. Funder strategy call landed the new map. USAID closed Mar 28. The funders are spooked.

**PNW Climate Week (Maritime track).** Bjorn Thorpe at Maritime Blue (J pod off Golden Gardens the day we met). Andy Gregory at Port of Seattle on the climate-priorities theme. Kelly DiNapoli at Bell Harbor Conference Center scoping July 18.

**SASD.** Started the entity. License and personal site still to do.

**The boat.** Inverter installed, propane locker, brackets, surveyor prep. 3D-print queue: hangers, hydroponic plant cup, cell-repeater mount.

**Mountains.** Spearhead Traverse prep mid-month: food and toe warmers and snow scouting at Elfin Lakes around Mar 22. Skiing with friends on a Friday. Sailing race with friends (cold, hectic; wear high boots while on the rail).

**Salt Spring.** Mar 27 ferry home with a friend after dinner at friends' on Salt Spring the night before. Pelmeni, long hot tub, cold plunge. We agreed to a social-media and external-input detox.

**Finds.** Mitch Ratcliffe on tools for stewardship. On a call with Bill Gates the same day. The Gates Foundation exploring Filecoin for distributed medical records. He's writing the 2040 plan for the Forest Service. A friend on digital transformation and donut economics. Jayant Menon on becoming PM/BD for Nahlia. Dinner with friends on Mar 28.

**Writing.** "A Path Into Sailing," Maker Primer, "How to consult a meeting," "How to start a climate crisis center," "Beyond Community Gardens": drafts. Start the actual blog. Reach the right people.

**Reading.** *Thinking in Systems*. *Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance*. *Story Worthy*. *The Naked Mind*. *1M Weekend*. *Your Next 5 Moves*. *A Most Remarkable Creature*.

**Lines.** A leader can grow to their level of inclusiveness; the lowest level is "me," the next is "us," then "we." Build the tools. Find the tool builders. Help the helpers. Art is creation in search of meaning. Mental tools are just as important as physical ones.

**Looking ahead.** I want spring to be tool builder (AOS), community organizer (Climate Weeks, climate nonprofit panel at the Exploratorium, a camp gathering), mountain runner (3x a week, WFA, Bowman with a friend). A Canada road trip on the table. Maybe Japan in the longer arc. Maybe a residence outside the US.
