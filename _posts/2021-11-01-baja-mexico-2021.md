The Baja Ha-Ha is the annual cruising rally down the outside of the Baja peninsula, San Diego to Cabo San Lucas, in a loose flotilla of a couple hundred boats. I sailed my Catalina 36, *Petrichor*, with crew from Oakland down the California coast, joined the rally start, and pushed south along Baja into the warmer water and the start of a season in Mexico.

First time captaining a major offshore passage in my own boat. The lessons piled up: provisioning for crew on a small boat, downwind sail combinations in the trades, dealing with mechanical issues when the next anchorage is still two days out.
