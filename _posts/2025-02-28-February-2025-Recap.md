Kate left Feb 1 for *Amundsen* and the Falklands. I stayed on Petrichor, started showing up at 9Zero, and ran ski crew calls from the boat.

## Projects

**Astraeus.** Feb 6 the founder equity meeting: Alex 50, me 20, Tanya 20, 10 reserved. The agreement: we pay ourselves when there are dollars per hour. The first concept-study offering started taking shape. Feb 18 standup talked methane leak detection (Picarro, Heath), tow-fish vs side-scan sonar, $50K to $120K per boat. Feb 24 a call with Aja Szumylo (15 years at NOAA, now the only employee at the Pacific Whiting Conservation Co-op) opened the bycatch-forecasting angle and put the Hake treaty meetings in Victoria on the March calendar.

**REAP.** Feb 11 we announced a $500K workforce-grant for Alameda and Contra Costa. By Feb 28 federal funds were freezing under the new administration. The pivot to family foundations (Schmidt Climate Futures, Pisces, East Bay, Silicon Valley Community, Founders Pledge) started in earnest. Kaitlin's warning landed: don't spend anything until we have a new strategy. I started doubting the model out loud.

**PNW Climate Week (Maritime track).** Picked it up with Bjorn Thorpe at Maritime Blue. Venue options: Bell Harbor Marina, Columbia Hospitality, World Trade Center Seattle. Feb 26 startup-track planning with Nigel Sharp and Zohar Ehrlich.

**Nahlia.** Jayant back from Galveston, good reception. 10 hours a week, $100 per hour.

**Mountains.** Feb 8 backcountry in Phantom Trees and Friendly Trees with Ajay, Sally, Nick Lee, Daniel Vashon. Dug a pit, ran a column test, skied three laps. Feb 2 day at Alpental with Davin ($165, ouch).

**Inner work.** Todd on Feb 10 introduced me to Internal Family Systems: *No Bad Parts*, *Self Therapy*, *You Are the One You've Been Waiting For*. I read all three. It changed how I think about my own internal politics.

**Family.** A visit from Mom and an honest letter draft about being dismissed. When you say I don't have trauma, it's discounting those experiences.

**Writing.** The Salt and Snow draft logs: shipping the boat from Mexico, storing in Ketchikan, the mice, the thru-hull material debate, the mongoose-and-watcher-bird metaphor for distrusting search engines.

**Lines.** If you have freedom, recognise it and use it. On REAP: the idea that we convert volunteers into donors is ridiculous. On the boat alone: it's cozy, but too easy to be a wanker there. I can't believe it's only been 3 weeks since Kate left, it's felt longer.

**Looking ahead.** PNW Climate Week buildout. Spearhead Traverse ski. SF Climate Week in April with Todd. Petrichor haul-out. Hake treaty in Victoria March 11-13. A real decision on REAP.
