## Van Isle 360

Three weeks on the boat with Beth, Holm, Kristin, Barend, and the crew. The boat is the heaviest in the fleet, also the most modern. Practice with Jake on Jun 1 was almost all upwind. Jun 2 the long dirt-road drive from Campbell River up to Mt. Cain via Quadra Island and Cafe Aroma, sleeping in the van. Around Jun 6-9 the Winter Harbour to Ucluelet leg: 140 miles, soft shackle failure, hourglassed kite. Slow is smooth. Do it right the first time. Ucluelet on Jun 10. Jun 12 Ucluelet to Victoria.
