# Library

## 2026

### Currently reading

- [*Uncivilisation: The Dark Mountain Manifesto*](https://dark-mountain.net/about/manifesto/). Notes pending.

### Finished

- [*The River Has Roots*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=The+River+Has+Roots+El-Mohtar), Amal El-Mohtar. Notes pending.
- [*Why Safeguarding Is Not Enough*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Why+Safeguarding+Is+Not+Enough+Sara+Roversi), Sara Roversi. Notes pending.
- Hannah Close's essays: *The Island*, [*On Skeleton Beach*](https://dark-mountain.net/on-skeleton-beach/). Read as Scotland prep. Notes pending.
- [*The Maintenance Race*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=The+Maintenance+Race+Stewart+Brand), Stewart Brand. One of my favorite pieces of writing. The first chapter on Robin Knox-Johnston and Donald Crowhurst is up there with Solnit's first chapter from *A Paradise Built in Hell*.
- [*Radical Universalism*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Radical+Universalism+Omri+Boehm), Omri Boehm. Complicated philosophy. The reminder that we have to pursue things people need now or they get frustrated and fight is worth the read.
- [*There Is No Antimemetics Division*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=There+Is+No+Antimemetics+Division+qntm), qntm. Sci-fi short novel about ideas and counter-ideas. Strong recommend. Four out of five.
- [*Circe*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Circe+Madeline+Miller), Madeline Miller. A reframing of the witch Circe. Loved Odysseus from her perspective as extremely egotistical. Highly recommend.
- [*Brigands and Breadknives*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Brigands+and+Breadknives+Travis+Baldree), Travis Baldree. Didn't go anywhere. Probably won't read more.
- [*Too Like the Lightning*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Too+Like+the+Lightning+Ada+Palmer), Ada Palmer. Machiavellian Florentine in the future, Shakespearean frame narrative. Wild. High three.
- [*Seven Surrenders*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Seven+Surrenders+Ada+Palmer), Ada Palmer. Notes pending.
- [*The Devils*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=The+Devils+Joe+Abercrombie), Joe Abercrombie. Typical D&D quest. Solid three.
- [*The Wonder Engine*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Wonder+Engine+T+Kingfisher), T. Kingfisher. Follow-up to *Clockwork Boys*. Solid three. Wouldn't really recommend.
- [*The Raven Scholar*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Raven+Scholar). Decent. 3.5. Better than a lot of others I've read. Will read the follow-up.
- [*City of Blades*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=City+of+Blades+Robert+Jackson+Bennett), Robert Jackson Bennett. Formulaic in a way that lets him explore the world rather than over-complicate the plot.
- [*City of Stairs*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=City+of+Stairs+Robert+Jackson+Bennett), Robert Jackson Bennett. See above.

## 2025

- [*A Court of Thorns and Roses*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=A+Court+of+Thorns+and+Roses+Maas), Sarah J. Maas. Read as a friend's romantasy challenge. Completely ridiculous but comically entertaining.
- [*Kushiel's Dart*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Kushiel's+Dart+Jacqueline+Carey), Jacqueline Carey. Much better written. Read one of the follow-ups.
- [*Some Desperate Glory*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Some+Desperate+Glory+Emily+Tesh), Emily Tesh. Solid four. About the radicalization of radicalized people. Aspire to Emily's research-then-write process.
- [*The Invisible Doctrine*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Invisible+Doctrine+Monbiot), George Monbiot. Neoliberalism's emergence. An okay book. Glad I read it.
- [*Things Become Other Things*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Things+Become+Other+Things+Craig+Mod), Craig Mod. Holding Craig as a staple. Will follow more of his work.
- [*The Book of Elsewhere*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Book+of+Elsewhere+Keanu+Reeves+Mieville), Keanu Reeves and China Mieville. Bizarre fantasy. Solid three.
- [*We Are Legion (We Are Bob)*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=We+Are+Legion+We+Are+Bob+Dennis+Taylor), Dennis E. Taylor. Brain uploading. Quite a lot of fun. Quick. 3.5.
- [*Penric and the Demon*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Penric+and+the+Demon+Bujold), Lois McMaster Bujold. Notes pending.
- [*The Curse of Chalion*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Curse+of+Chalion+Bujold), Lois McMaster Bujold. Notes pending.
- [*Bookshops & Bonedust*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Bookshops+and+Bonedust+Baldree), Travis Baldree. Notes pending.
- [*Legends & Lattes*](https://www.goodreads.com/search?q=Legends+and+Lattes+Baldree), Travis Baldree. Cute, a cafe in a city. Notes pending.
