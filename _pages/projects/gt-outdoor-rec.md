# Georgia Tech Outdoor Recreation

<!-- Photo slot: drop a file at /images/projects/gt-outdoor-rec.jpg, then delete the comment markers on the next line. -->
<!-- <img src="/images/projects/gt-outdoor-rec.jpg" width="100%" alt="A Georgia Tech outdoor program trip"> -->

While at Georgia Tech (2006-2011), I was the Student Director of Outdoor Recreation Georgia Tech (ORGT), working alongside the professional staff. ORGT runs the campus caving, climbing, whitewater kayaking, and backpacking programs, and I coordinated across all of them: the trips, the gear, the safety, and the fundraising and logistics that kept them going. It was where the organizing-and-logistics muscle that runs through everything since first got built.

## What I ran

As Student Director I sat between the student community and the professional staff, coordinating the separate outdoor disciplines, organizing trips, and handling the fundraising and logistics behind them.
