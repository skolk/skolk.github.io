# The Bus

<!-- Photo slot: drop a file at /images/projects/the-bus.jpg, then delete the comment markers on the next line. -->
<!-- <img src="/images/projects/the-bus.jpg" width="100%" alt="The bus built out into a livable RV"> -->

In 2011, a few of us bought a school bus, named her *Bessy*, and built her out into a livable RV: bunk beds, couches, a real interior. Then we drove her west to Yellowstone by way of Moab, and up to the Boundary Waters. An early build-and-go project in the same spirit as the [Halfway House]({% post_url 2016-08-15-tiny-house-post %}) tiny house that came later. *Bessy* grew out of the same community as the [Friday gatherings]({{ '/projects/friday-gatherings/' | prepend: site.baseurl }}): one of the things that came out of just showing up every week was a group buying a bus together.

## The build

Turning *Bessy* into a place you can actually live and travel in: berths, seating, and the systems that make a mobile home work.

## The trip

West to Yellowstone through Moab, then north to the Boundary Waters.
